Why VR for rehabilitation?

Through testing it has been shown that the rehabilitation process is much faster when using Virtual Reality as a tool. Two groups got tested, one using Virtual Reality as a rehabilitation tool and the other group used traditional rehabilitation methods.

After two months the results showed that the Virtual Reality group had improved their motor functions better than the other group.
